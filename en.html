We were brought together by truth and love, for which we would like to kindly express our thanks to the Forum 2000 Foundation. During the Forum 2000 party, held in autumn 2009, we talked, drank, fasted and danced together for the first time and within the Foundation we kept meeting casually as well as in terms of work (we still haven't settled who was whose boss at one particular time... Zuzka!). That all led to us meeting at the Kecejme do toho festival held in Prague park Parukářka, where my scarf found its way into Martins back pack by chance (Zuzka!). Our first official date took place at the night of full moon before another Forum 2000 conference. Thanks to the busy social life, general workload and a convenient adress in the city centre, our relationship heightened rapidly and we lived together in a cosy studio.

 

We are married since 14. 8. 2014.
